# Privacy Policy for SelfCare

**App:** SelfCare
**Developer:** [Your Company]
**Effective date:** [YYYY-MM-DD]

This Privacy Policy describes how the SelfCare mobile application ("SelfCare", "we", "us", or "our") handles information when you use the app. We have designed SelfCare to be private by default. Your personal wellness information stays on your device.

## Summary

- We do not run servers, accounts, or cloud sync for your wellness data.
- Your tasks, mood entries, journal entries, study sessions, and settings are stored locally on your device.
- The app shows ads through Google AdMob. AdMob is the only third-party component that may receive limited device-level information for the purpose of serving ads.

## Data We Collect

SelfCare does not collect, transmit, or store your personal mood entries, tasks, journal entries, or study session data on any server. All of this content is written to a local database (Hive) on your device. We cannot read it, we cannot back it up for you, and we cannot recover it if it is lost.

We do not ask for an email address, phone number, name, or any other identifying information to use the app.

## Advertising

SelfCare integrates the Google AdMob SDK to display banner, native, interstitial, and (optionally) rewarded ads. AdMob is operated by Google and may collect certain information for the purpose of serving and measuring ads, including:

- A resettable mobile advertising identifier (such as the Android Advertising ID)
- Coarse device information (device type, operating system version, language)
- Approximate location derived from your IP address
- Interaction data with ads (such as ad views and taps)

Google may use this information to provide personalized or non-personalized ads, to measure ad performance, to detect fraud and abuse, and for the other purposes described in Google's policies. We do not have access to the raw data Google collects and we cannot match it to your in-app content.

For details on how Google handles this information, please review:

- Google Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy
- How Google uses information from sites or apps that use our services: https://policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites

## Permissions

SelfCare requests the following device permissions:

- **Notifications.** Used to deliver local reminders for tasks you schedule and for daily check-in nudges. Reminders are scheduled on the device; they are not pushed from a server.
- **Exact alarms** (on Android versions that require it). Used to deliver task reminders at the precise time you select.
- **Network access.** Used by the AdMob SDK to request ads. SelfCare itself does not transmit your wellness content over the network.

You may revoke any of these permissions in your device settings at any time. Revoking notification permission will disable reminders. Revoking network access will prevent ads from loading but will not otherwise affect the app.

## Children

SelfCare is a general-audience wellness app and is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided information to the app, please uninstall the app to remove all locally stored data.

## Your Choices

- **Delete all data.** Because everything is stored locally, uninstalling SelfCare removes all of your tasks, mood entries, journal entries, study sessions, and settings from your device.
- **Opt out of personalized ads.** Android lets you reset or limit your Advertising ID under *Settings > Privacy > Ads* (the exact path varies by device). When you opt out, AdMob will serve non-personalized ads instead of personalized ones.
- **Disable ads-related network traffic.** Turning off network access for SelfCare will prevent ad requests. The rest of the app will continue to work offline.

## Data Security

Because your data never leaves your device, the security of that data depends on the security of your device. We recommend using a device lock (PIN, pattern, biometric) and keeping your operating system up to date. SelfCare relies on the platform-provided sandboxing offered by Android to isolate its data from other apps.

## Data Retention

Local data is retained on your device until you delete an entry inside the app or until you uninstall the app. We do not retain copies of your content because we never receive it.

## International Users

SelfCare is designed to work the same way everywhere because it does not transfer your personal content across borders. AdMob may transfer ad-related information to servers operated by Google in various countries; please see Google's privacy policy for details on cross-border transfers.

Material changes will also be reflected in the app's store listing. Your continued use of SelfCare after an update means you accept the revised policy.
